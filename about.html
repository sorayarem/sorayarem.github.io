About Me

When I’m not on my computer or contemplating the work that I should be doing at my computer, I love to spend my free time reading. My goal of 90 books on Goodreads this year is my loftiest yet, but my latest challenge for myself has helped me find some great new reads.

I was born and raised in Northern Virginia, but I love to travel and explore new cities both within and outside the US, and I’m always on the hunt for good gluten-free food wherever I go. Keep scrolling to see a bit more!
