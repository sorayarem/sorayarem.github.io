Problem Statement

Women on campus need a safer and easier way to get around at night because traditional ride-share apps like Uber and Lyft can cause safety concerns.

Affinity Diagram

This diagram lays out the major points of the proposed app, with the basic functions of the app laid out for both the driver and the rider. Clearly, the app needs to be able to handle the need of both and use location and pricing features to make the exchange between driver and rider as seamless as possible. However, as with any app, there are restrictions and limitations that one encounters. Since this is a smaller business, it comes into direct competition with other companies like Uber and Lyft, and needs to have a competitive or non-existent price point to be effective in gaining customers. Additionally, an app is preferable to a website in this instance, as apps are more practical for the target age group in this scenario.
